Thriftsburgh

Thriftsburgh is a student-run on- campus thrift store, where you can donate clothes and shoes. By selling second-hand clothing, they reduce textile wast, cuts carbon, and promotes a culture of reuse.

Pollinator gardens are used to attract pollinators. Pollinators are integral to the growth of 75 - 95% of Earth's plants. 40% of the pollinator population is decreasing which may lead to less biodiversity and make more plants go extinct. By building more of these gardens with bee houses, we may be able to stop this decrease.
